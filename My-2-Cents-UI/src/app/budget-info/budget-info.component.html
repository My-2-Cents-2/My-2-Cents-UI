50 - 30 - 20 Rule


This budget calculator is based on the 50 - 30 - 20 rule of thumb.

Essentially, this rule breaks your income down into percentages that represent a category

Your Needs are equal to 50% percent of your total income.

Needs qualify as things you cannot live without like groceries, mortgage, insurance,

While your Wants are equal to 30 percent of your income and would be eating out,

hobbies, vaction, or shopping

Finally, Savings is equal to 20 percent so your 401K, mutual funds, index funds

or however you chose to save your money
